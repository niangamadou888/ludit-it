Human Factors

The Human Factors sector is specialised in studies of Human-Machine Interaction and Human Factors training in technical organisations. The experience in the field covers several training courses developed over the years for airline companies and air traffic control organisations, in collaboration with the European Commission, private airlines and Eurocontrol.
